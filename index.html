About

My self

I'm a person who loves building things with code.

I have become more enthralled with programming from 3rd year at my university as one of our instructors demonstrated the practical use of programming in many fields. After the demonstration, I fell in love with the testing and debugging using MATLAB in my Digital Image Processing subject and building "Image Enhancement Tool" for my B.E. thesis. And, in my master's theses for the Master's Double Degree Program, I've used C++ and MCF for my master's theses at universities.

After I'd finished my master's courses, the COVID-19 pandemic hit suddenly and I had more time to explore the updates and trending IT tech news for career choices in the IT sector. And then, I specifically learned web development as I had things that I wanted to build for myself.

After a few months of self-teaching, I knew this was what I wanted to do for my career. Since then, I've grown up as a developer. I'm now seeking a full-time role where I can contribute my skills in coding to help a company achieve their goals.
